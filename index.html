Welcome to SCCM Child Care

At SCCM Child Care our goal is to provide a safe, caring, and fun environment where your child can learn, play, and grow. We understand that each child has unique talents, learning styles, and personalities. Here you will find a safe & nurturing environment for children of all ages.

While enrolled at SCCM Child Care your child will participate in developmentally-appropriate activities, such as: Preschool Curriculum, Field Trips, Outside Play, Story Time, Sand & Water Play, Fun Fridays, Arts and Crafts, Music and Dance, and much more...

At SCCM Family Child Care we feel it is important for parents to know how their child spent the day. Each day your child will take home a "What I Did Today" sheet. We provide healthy meals and snacks for each child. We also send home monthly newsletters which keep parents informed about curriculum, field trips, and other fun activites.

We are licensed as a large family child care home.
We meet and exceed all health and safety guidelines.

Thank you for taking the time to visit our site and for your interest in SCCM Child Care.
